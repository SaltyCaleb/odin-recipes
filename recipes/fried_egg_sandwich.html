Simple Fried Egg Sandwich

The fried egg sandwich is a perfect healthy breakfast option to learn how to cook as a complete beginner. Many toppings can also be added to increase calories and flavor, such as sausage, bacon, ham, scrapple, tomatoe and/or letttuce.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 8 slices of toasted white bread
  • 2 tablespoons mayonaise
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • salt and pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium high heat and add butter
  2. Crack eggs into pan and cook to desired firmness. Just before eggs are finished, place a slice of cheese over each one.
  3. Transfer each egg to a toasted slice of bread and season accordingly with the salt and pepper
  4. Spread mayonaise and ketchup on remaining bread slices
  5. Cover the bread slices with egg with the slices that have the ketchup-mayonaise mixture to make 4 fried egg sandwiches. Enjoy!
